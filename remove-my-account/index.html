Account Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account in our game StonksFarmer, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to gregoire.lecun@gmail.com with the subject line "StonksFarmer Account Deletion".
  2. In the email, please include the email address associated with your game account.

We are committed to deleting your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Data types that will be deleted: Email, password, game history.
